Laboratory

Step into Life Care Hospitals' labs, a hub of precise and swift testing. At Life Care, your wellness is our responsibility. Life Care Hospitals' Medical Laboratory Service has expanded significantly, across three districts. Our team of highly trained and qualified laboratory staff, technicians, and consultants are dedicated to providing superior service, ensuring our customers receive accurate and reliable laboratory results. Focusing on utilizing state-of-the-art technology, Hematology, HPLC, and Electrolyte Analyzers work into our laboratory system to minimize human intervention and reduce report turnaround time. This technology meets global laboratory technology standards and the stringent requirements of modern medicine and healthcare. In addition, we conduct routine internal quality control tests on our machines and are committed to maintaining the highest quality standards in our laboratory tests.
