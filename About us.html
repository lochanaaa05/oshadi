About us

Kalutara Life Care

The Life Care Hospitals - Kalutara branch is perfectly situated right in the heart of the town of Mathugama. The facility is dedicated to serving customers in the locale and providing them with a full set of medical services at affordable prices.

Piliyandala Life Care

Commenced in the year 2017, Life Care Hospitals - Piliyandala is a modern healthcare facility that has been designed and developed in accordance with world-class standards and it includes a range of contemporary features. The facility primarily serves people from the town of Piliyandala and its surrounding suburbs, offering them a range of high-quality healthcare services.

Kandy Life Care

Life Care Hospitals - Kandy started operations in 2018 and it provides a complete set of modern-day healthcare services to individuals from the locale of Moratuwa and the surrounding suburbs. The facility is fully equipped with the most advanced medical technology, which allows us to offer more effective and efficient healthcare services while transforming the patient and family experience.

Chairman's Message

Greetings from our Team to all our friends, partners and valued members in the community,

I am very privileged to welcome you here as the Chairman of the organisation “LIFE CARE Hospitals” with complete commitment. Our mission has always been clear: to produce efficient and quality healthcare services that can not only cure diseases but also promote holistic health of people who come to us for their treatment.

At “LIFE CARE Hospitals” we measure health in terms of physical, mental, and social well-being of a person. This is well illustrated in our medical professionals who are not only well experienced but also posses the ability to show the human side in handling patients with different ailments.

In fact, putting into consideration the importance of the patients' experience in the health care facility is the driving force of our institution. Our goal is to ensure that all the patient feels valued, acknowledged, and accompanied through out his/her treatment period. In incorporating everything that is current in the medical field and in improving the services offered, the patients get the best quality health care as it is available in the market.

People with disabilities are central to our company and value as collaboration and partnership are essential. We extend our sincere appreciation to all the medical professionals, universities and other organizations together with the whole community who have collaborated with us in order to move forward and ensure that the healthcare standards are achieved. In combination, we are realising the creation of an effective and adaptive healthcare service delivery mechanism that will suit the existing and emerging needs of people.

Although the future may be uncertain, what remains clear to me is commitment of our medical team, loyalty of our patients, and robust partnerships we enjoy. All together we are working for to fulfill our goals that provide the benchmark for LIFE CARE as a symbol of hope and health care.

Once again this has been an honor to have such an important part in the construction of your life. There is nothing much to say but let us join hands once again in projecting the light towards a healthier and a more optimistic future.

With heartfelt gratitude,

Ms. M. Oshadi Ayodhya
